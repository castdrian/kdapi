Introduction

Able (Korean: 에이블) is a South Korean singer under Donuts Culture. She is a member of the duo A+B and a former member of the group Maywish as Jelly (제령).

On October 17, 2018, she debuted as a member of Maywish under J9 Entertainment using the stage name Jelly. She left both the company and group in June 2019.

After leaving J9 Entertainment, she signed with Donuts Culture.

On June 28, 2022, she debuted in the duo A+B under the stage name Able with fellow soloist by me.
