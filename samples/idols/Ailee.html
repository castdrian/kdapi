Introduction

Ailee is a South Korean-American singer-songwriter under A2Z Entertainment. Based in South Korea, she debuted on February 8, 2012, with the single "Heaven".On January 7, 2017, she made her American debut with the single "Fall Back" under the stage name A.Leean.

In September 2019, Ailee started a new agency with her team called Rocket3 Entertainment. On July 22, 2021, she became the first artist to sign under The L1ve. She later left The L1ve and announced that she joined POP MUSIC on July 30, 2022.
