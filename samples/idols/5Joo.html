Introduction

5Joo is a South Korean singer and streamer, she is a former member and the Main Vocalist of the girl group ikling under CodeM Entertainment.

She debuted as a soloist with the mini album "Let’s Go To See The Flowers" on August 14, 2019. She debuted in the project girl group, ikling on April 28, 2023 with the release of their first digital single, "Stargram". She then left the group before their line up change.
