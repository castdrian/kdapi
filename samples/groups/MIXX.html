Introduction

MIXX was a five-member girl group under Chiko Bros Entertainment. They debuted on May 3, 2016, with the single "Oh Ma Mind".

The group started with four members in 2016: Hanna, Henny, Arie, and Liyah. Henny left sometime in 2016 and two new members, Heeu and Mia, were added in 2017.

MIXX disbanded on March 17, 2017, due to the THAAD issue between China and Korea, with the Chinese members returning to their home country.
